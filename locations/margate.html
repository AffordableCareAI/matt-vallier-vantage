Insurance Coverage for Margate Residents

Margate is a diverse and welcoming community in the heart of Broward County, home to more than 58,000 residents. Bordered by Coral Springs to the west and Coconut Creek to the north, Margate offers an affordable alternative to neighboring cities while maintaining easy access to all of Broward County's healthcare providers and hospitals. From the neighborhoods around Oriole Golf and Tennis Club to the communities along State Road 7, Margate residents represent a wide range of ages, backgrounds, and insurance needs.

Matthew Vallier at Vantage Insurance Holdings serves Margate residents from the nearby Coral Springs office, just a short drive down Sample Road or State Road 7. As an independent agent with access to 17 carriers, Matthew is not limited to showing you one company's products. He compares Medicare Advantage plans, Medicare Supplement policies, ACA health insurance options, life insurance, annuities, and supplemental coverage from Aetna, Humana, UnitedHealthcare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, and more to find the plan that fits your situation.

Margate's population includes a significant number of Medicare-eligible residents, many of whom moved to the area for its affordability and proximity to Broward County's medical facilities. Whether you are newly eligible for Medicare, looking to switch plans during Annual Enrollment, or need life insurance to protect your family, Matthew provides honest guidance without the high-pressure tactics you might find elsewhere.
