About Vantage Insurance Holdings

An independent agency built on trust, transparency, and doing right by every client we serve across Southeast Florida.

Our Story

Building Trust Across Southeast Florida

Matthew Vallier founded Vantage Insurance Holdings with a simple conviction: insurance should protect people, not confuse them. As owner and principal agent, Matt has built an agency that is known and respected throughout Southeast Florida for putting clients first.

Based in Coral Springs, Vantage serves families and individuals across Broward and Palm Beach counties with a full spectrum of coverage options. Matt’s deep expertise spans Medicare Supplements, Medicare Advantage, ACA health insurance, life insurance, and supplemental products—giving clients access to the right plan at the right price, regardless of which carrier offers it.

“Insurance isn’t something people want to think about—until they need it. My job is to make sure that when that moment comes, you’re covered. No gaps, no surprises.”

As an independent agency, Vantage isn’t tied to any single carrier. Matt shops 17+ carriers to find coverage that matches each client’s health needs, doctors, prescriptions, and budget. That independence is the foundation of everything Vantage does.

Our Mission

“Bad things happen to good people.”

That’s the reality that drives Vantage Insurance Holdings every single day. Accidents, illnesses, unexpected loss—these things don’t discriminate. They happen to hardworking families, to retirees who planned carefully, to young professionals just getting started. When life takes an unexpected turn, insurance is the safety net that catches you.

Vantage exists to make sure that safety net is strong, affordable, and tailored to your life. As an independent agency, we work for you—not the insurance carriers. We compare plans across 17+ carriers to find coverage that actually fits, at a price that makes sense. No loyalty to any one company. No quotas. Just honest guidance from a licensed professional who knows the market inside and out.

When you work with Vantage, you get a partner who picks up the phone, explains things in plain English, and fights to make sure you’re never left uncovered when it matters most.

Matthew Vallier is the owner and driving force behind Vantage Insurance Holdings. With deep expertise across Medicare, ACA marketplace plans, life insurance, final expense, annuities, and supplemental coverage, Matt brings the kind of comprehensive knowledge that most agents simply don’t have.

Licensed in six states—27 states across the country—Matt serves clients from Southeast Florida’s coastal communities to rural counties across the Southeast. His approach is education-first: he makes sure every client fully understands their options before making a single recommendation.

Matt is known for his patience, his attention to detail, and his willingness to go the extra mile. Whether it’s explaining the difference between Medicare Supplement plans, shopping multiple carriers for the most competitive life insurance rate, or helping a client navigate a complex health situation, Matt treats every case like it’s his own family’s coverage on the line.
